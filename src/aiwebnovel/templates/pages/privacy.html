Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

What We Collect

When you use SloppyRoad, we collect:

  • Account info: Email address, pen name, hashed password
  • Generated content: Novels, chapters, world data, character profiles, images you create through the platform
  • Usage data: LLM API costs, generation history, reading progress, time-on-page metrics
  • Technical data: IP address (for rate limiting and security), browser type, request logs

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking scripts
  • We do not sell your data to advertisers
  • We do not share your email with marketing partners

How We Use Your Data

  • Service operation: Generating content, tracking budgets, sending notifications
  • Security: Rate limiting, fraud prevention, authentication
  • Communication: Email verification, password reset, service announcements

Third-Party Services

To generate content, we send your creative prompts and story context to:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — Text generation
  • OpenAI (GPT) — Text generation
  • Replicate (Flux) — Image generation
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery

Each provider has their own privacy policy. We send only the minimum data necessary for generation (story context, prompts). We do not send your email, password, or account info to LLM providers.

BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)

If you provide your own API keys, they are encrypted with Fernet authenticated encryption and stored in our database. Keys are decrypted only when making API calls on your behalf. We never log, display, or share your full API keys.

Cookies

We use two cookies:

  • aiwn_token: Session authentication (JWT, HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax)
  • csrf_token: Cross-site request forgery protection

We do not use advertising, analytics, or social media tracking cookies.

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Generated content (novels, chapters, images) is retained until you delete the novel or your account
  • Usage logs are retained for 90 days for billing reconciliation
  • Server request logs are retained for 7 days for debugging

Data Deletion

You can delete individual novels from your dashboard. Account deletion is available in Settings — this anonymizes your account data and marks all your novels as private.

Security

We use:

  • HTTPS everywhere (TLS 1.3)
  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Fernet encryption for API keys
  • CSRF protection on all forms
  • Rate limiting on all endpoints
  • Security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options)

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your account data through the dashboard
  • Update your email, pen name, and password in Settings
  • Delete your novels and generated content
  • Delete your account entirely
  • Contact us with questions about your data

Contact

Privacy questions? Email privacy@sloppyroad.com.
